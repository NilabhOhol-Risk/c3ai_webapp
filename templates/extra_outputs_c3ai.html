Activity Levels

School activities for key contact children are categorized as:

Label Description of school activity
School 1 In-person K-12 school complying with recognized social precautions
School 2 In-person K-12 school with near-normal Normal activity levels correspond to pre-pandemic activity without compliance with social precautions. activities

Occupation activities Key contact adults participate in an occupation, such as employment or post-secondary school or other activities during work hours. for key contact adults are categorized as:

Label Description of occupation activity
Work 0 Online occupation
Work 1 In-person occupation complying with recognized social precautions
Work 2 In-person occupation with near-normal Normal activity levels correspond to pre-pandemic activity without compliance with social precautions. activities

Community activities All key contacts may additionally participate in community activities outside of school/occupation. for all key contacts are categorized as:

Label Description of occupation activity
Community 0 Community activities at 0% of normal: Maintain all recognized social precautions
Community 25 25% of community activities without maintaining recognized social precautions
Community 50 50% of community activities without maintaining recognized social precautions
Community 75 75% of community activities without maintaining recognized social precautions
Community 100 Community activities at 100% of normalNormal activity levels correspond to pre-pandemic activity without compliance with social precautions.: Pre-pandemic normal level
